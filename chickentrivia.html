Did you know this about chickens

1. There are more chickens than any other bird or poultry in the world with a population of over 19 billion chickens.

2.Chickens descended from red jungle fowl a type of wild poultry.

3. In 2011/2012 average chicken consumption was 14.5 kg per person per year. This is 276 million kg eaten per day translating to roughly 55 million chickens consumed per day.
